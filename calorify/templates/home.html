Welcome to Calorify

What are we?

A website designed to help you figure out all the nutritional facts of what you eat.

How does it work?

To begin, simply upload a picture of your food or enter the image URL. Based on AI machine learning, Clarifai's Food Model will help determine what the food is using image recognition. It will then pass the food items through Edamam's Food Database which will essentially give you all the nutritional information of the food shown in the picture.
